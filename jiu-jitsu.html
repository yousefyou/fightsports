Jiu-Jitsu

There are two different forms of this sport: Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and Japanese Jiu-Jitsu. The attack techniques are the throw techniques, the grip techniques, clamping techniques and the kick techniques. These techniques are really dangerous. They could break your bones or knock you out. A fight could be end in a few seconds via a knock-out or a submission.
