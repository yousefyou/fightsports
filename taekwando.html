Taekwondo

This sport is also a self-defense sport. It is mostly kicking and a little bit punching. The trainings are based on techniques, sparring and style-forms. A fight could be end via a knockdown or a knock-out. There are a few things which are important in Taekwondo:

courtesy, self-control, perseverance, integrity, enormous mental power and focus.

It's an olympic sport since 1973.
