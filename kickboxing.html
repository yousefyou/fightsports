Kickboxing

This sport is known over the whole world. It has born in Thailand. The best fighters come from Thailand and The Netherlands. The greatest kickboxing organizations are GLORY, Enfusion and K1.

Kickboxers are divided over three classes:
C-class, B-class and A-class. The length of a contest is three rounds of three minutes. A fight could be end with a knock-out or repeated knockdowns.
